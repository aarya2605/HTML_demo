P.V. Sindhu

Indian Badminton Player and Olympic Medalist

Early Life

P.V. Sindhu was born on July 5, 1995, in Hyderabad, India.
She comes from a sports-oriented family; her father, P.V. Ramana, is a former volleyball player and her mother, P. Vijaya, is a former national level volleyball player.

Career Highlights

  • Gold Medal at the 2019 World Championships
  • Silver Medal at the 2016 Rio Olympics
  • Bronze Medal at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics
  • Numerous titles in the BWF tournaments

Achievements

P.V. Sindhu has received numerous awards, including:

  1. Padma Bhushan (2016)
  2. Padma Shri (2015)
  3. Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna (2016)

Personal Life

Sindhu is known for her dedication and hard work. She trains rigorously and has become a role model for aspiring athletes in India. Apart from badminton, she enjoys traveling and watching movies.
